Pricing Questions

Are there hidden fees?

No. What we quote is what you pay. We're transparent about all costs upfront. No surprise fees, no hidden charges.

Can I get a quote first?

Yes! We'll review your documents and give you a free quote before we start any work. No obligation.

What if my return is more complex?

We'll discuss any additional costs before we start. If your situation is more complex, we'll explain why and get your approval first.

Do you charge for e-filing?

No. E-filing is included in our service. We don't charge extra to file your return electronically.

When do I pay?

You pay after we prepare your return and you approve it. We don't file until you're satisfied and approve the final price.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, check, and credit/debit cards. Payment plans available for larger returns.
