What Documents Do I Need?

W2s from all employers

1099s (if you're self-employed or have other income)

Social Security numbers for you, spouse, and dependents

Last year's tax return (if you have it)

Receipts for deductions (charitable donations, medical expenses, etc.)

Bank account info for direct deposit

Any IRS letters you received

How Long Does It Take?

Simple returns: Same day or next day

Complex returns: 2-3 business days

During tax season: May take slightly longer due to volume

E-filing: IRS typically processes in 1-3 weeks

Refund: Usually arrives in 2-3 weeks after e-filing

Can You File Electronically?

Yes. We e-file all returns when possible.

Faster processing than paper filing

More accurate - fewer errors

Faster refunds - usually 2-3 weeks

No extra cost - e-filing is included

Confirmation - you get proof of filing
